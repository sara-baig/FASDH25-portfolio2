Is Israel a rogue state?

-----

Shireen Abu Akleh was a household name across the Middle East.
The veteran Al Jazeera journalist earned widespread praise for more than two decades of reporting on Israel’s occupation of Palestinian territory and covering stories of war, atrocities, and Palestinian resistance.
She was shot in the head as she reported on Israel’s latest raid on the Jenin refugee camp, in the occupied West Bank.
Will Israel get away with its crimes?
And who can hold Israel to account?
Presenter: Hashem Ahelbarra
Guests:
Hagai El-Ad – Executive director of The Israeli Information Center for Human Rights in the Occupied Territories (B’Tselem)
Aicha Elbasri – Researcher at the Arab Center for Research and Policy Studies
Rami Khouri – Professor of journalism at the American University of Beirut and senior fellow at Harvard Kennedy School
