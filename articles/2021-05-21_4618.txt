Is US opinion shifting on the Israel-Palestine conflict?

-----

The United States has traditionally been Israel’s most important ally, but American politicians and the public are increasingly challenging Washington’s longstanding and unquestioned loyalty.
Progressive Democrats pressured President Joe Biden to demand a ceasefire on Gaza, and they want weapons sales to Israel to stop.
Recent polls show Americans, including younger American Jews, are showing more support for the Palestinians.
How will this shift affect future ties between the two nations?
